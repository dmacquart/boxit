Frequently Asked Questions

How many boxes can I store?

As many as you want. Start with 1, add more anytime. We'll send you more labels when you run out.

What if I need my stuff back quickly?

Request delivery through your portal. We'll drop it off within 2 business days, usually sooner.

What size boxes do I need?

Use any boxes you want - moving boxes, Amazon boxes, plastic bins, whatever. Just make sure you can lift them (our drivers appreciate it).

Is there a weight limit?

Keep boxes under 50 lbs each. If it's heavier, just use two boxes.

What can't I store?

No perishables, hazardous materials, or anything illegal. Otherwise, pack what you need.

When do I get charged?

Your card is saved at signup but not charged until your first box is picked up. After that, you're billed monthly for membership + storage, plus one-time fees for additional pickups/deliveries.

What if I need to cancel?

Cancel anytime after your first 6 months. We'll deliver your remaining boxes for free.

Do you serve outside Oakland?

Currently serving Oakland, Berkeley, and Emeryville. Live nearby? Email us - we're expanding.
